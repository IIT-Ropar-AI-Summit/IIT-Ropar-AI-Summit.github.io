About AI Summit

Pre-AI Summit, hosted by the School of Artificial Intelligence and Data Engineering (sAIDE), IIT Ropar, is a precursor to the national-level AI Summit to be organized by the Government of India and aims to bring together students, researchers, and practitioners to discuss emerging trends, challenges, and opportunities in artificial intelligence.
